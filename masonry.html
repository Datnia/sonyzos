I’m Sonia, a graphic designer and illustrator living in the DMV area. I graduated from the University of Maryland in 2016 with a BA in Studio Art, concentrating in Graphic Design. Some previous clients of mine include Stanford, National Wildlife Federation, World Justice Project and many other wonderful non-profits and people passionate about the world we live in today.

Currently I’m working as a contractor and freelancer. I’m looking for remote work opportunities that cover a wide array of design work including: UX/UI, editorial, print and product design. Collaborating, starting new projects, or just a simple conversation are some hobbies of mine, so hit me up and let’s talk! If we’re in the same area, let’s sit down and have a cup of coffee.

The International Union of Bricklayers and Allied Craftworkers represent craftworkers in the trowel trades across the United States and Canada. BAC is a gateway to education programs that support members as craftworkers interested in expanding their career options. For this year's career fair, I designed theme graphics that would be transferred to a trade booth, takeaway postcard and SWAG items.

takeaway

Along with the booth, banner and SWAG items, I designed a postcard takeaway for career fair attendees. The front side replicates the look, feel and messaging on the banners and the backside has more information about the apprenticeship program. I went with a graphic treatment to highlight wage rates, crafts listed and apprenticeship contact information.
