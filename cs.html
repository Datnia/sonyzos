happy you're here. i'm sonia and i thought i'd tell you a bit more about myself.

I’m Sonia, a graphic designer and illustrator living in the DMV area. I graduated from the University of Maryland in 2016 with a BA in Studio Art, concentrating in Graphic Design. Some previous clients of mine include Stanford, National Wildlife Federation, World Justice Project and many other wonderful non-profits and people passionate about the world we live in today.

Currently I’m working as a contractor and freelancer. I’m looking for remote work opportunities that cover a wide array of design work including: UX/UI, editorial, print and product design. Collaborating, starting new projects, or just a simple conversation are some hobbies of mine, so hit me up and let’s talk! If we’re in the same area, let’s sit down and have a cup of coffee.

presentation

Capitol Standard is an online magazine offering an upwardly mobile and distinctly powerful audience a fresh perspective on their careers and the lifestyle that surrounds it. As the only illustrator and designer on the team at that time, I had the responsibility of establishing a youthful illustration style and keeping graphics on brand.

illustration pack

I've created an illustration pack that will come in handy for future articles. These are mixed media: digital illustration work with realistic, flat patterns. Look closely and you'll find them!

personalities of dc

One of the most popular articles at Capitol Standard is "The 6 Types of People You Meet In DC." To visualize these personalities, I illustrated a character for each. All characters are of different races, gender and religion. My #1 priority in my illustration work is representation.

portrait illustrations

A collection of portrait illustrations that follow the brand guidelines of: loose-line work, muted colors and watercolor treatment. As an illustrator for the magazine, I was in charge of creating the first fine art works for many magazine articles.
