happy you're here, thought i'd tell you a bit more about myself.

I’m Sonia, a graphic designer and illustrator living in the DMV area. I graduated from the University of Maryland in 2016 with a BA in Studio Art, concentrating in Graphic Design. Some previous clients of mine include Stanford, National Wildlife Federation, World Justice Project and many other wonderful non-profits and people passionate about the world we live in today.

Currently I’m working as a contractor and freelancer. I’m looking for remote work opportunities that cover a wide array of design work including: UX/UI, editorial, print and product design. Collaborating, starting new projects, or just a simple conversation are some hobbies of mine, so hit me up and let’s talk! If we’re in the same area, let’s sit down and have a cup of coffee.
