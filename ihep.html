happy you're here, thought i'd tell you a bit more about myself.

I’m Sonia, a graphic designer and illustrator living in the DMV area. I graduated from the University of Maryland in 2016 with a BA in Studio Art, concentrating in Graphic Design. Some previous clients of mine include Stanford, National Wildlife Federation, World Justice Project and many other wonderful non-profits and people passionate about the world we live in today.

Currently I’m working as a contractor and freelancer. I’m looking for remote work opportunities that cover a wide array of design work including: UX/UI, editorial, print and product design. Collaborating, starting new projects, or just a simple conversation are some hobbies of mine, so hit me up and let’s talk! If we’re in the same area, let’s sit down and have a cup of coffee.

The Institute for Higher Education Policy (IHEP) is a nonpartisan, nonprofit organization committed to promoting access to and success in higher education for all students. Looking for a fresh new look, I used their existing logo to re-create their web and print branding. Rebranded materials include a report template, letterhead, folder and many other print documents.

ihep reports

A huge part of IHEP's work is research and analysis geared toward policymaking and general audiences. IHEP produces a number of professional reports and papers each year that explore a variety of higher education related topics. I redesigned the report covers and interior pages, creating a clean template that can be used for all future research.

anniversary logo

I also designed their 25th anniversary logo, which is currently used on their homepage. The lines that cut through the 25 mirror the line thickness and direction of the main logo. These work as a package that can be used together.
