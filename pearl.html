happy you're here, thought i'd tell you a bit more about myself.

I’m Sonia, a graphic designer and illustrator living in the DMV area. I graduated from the University of Maryland in 2016 with a BA in Studio Art, concentrating in Graphic Design. Some previous clients of mine include Stanford, National Wildlife Federation, World Justice Project and many other wonderful non-profits and people passionate about the world we live in today.

Currently I’m working as a contractor and freelancer. I’m looking for remote work opportunities that cover a wide array of design work including: UX/UI, editorial, print and product design. Collaborating, starting new projects, or just a simple conversation are some hobbies of mine, so hit me up and let’s talk! If we’re in the same area, let’s sit down and have a cup of coffee.

Pearl Contracting is a construction and home management start-up company based in Silver Spring, Maryland. I worked one-on-one with owner and developer to create a look that represents the hard work that’s put into the company every day. This project includes logo design, web design and additional branding materials.

web design and direction

I worked together with a developer to create a responesive, one page website that can be used on all platforms. This is a brief overview of the services and projects provided, followed by a contact form. Bright colors, subtle animation and unique icons were emphasized to set Pearl Contracting apart from the traditional contracting website.

branding and print

I designed both a main logo (which can be seen on the business cards) and a logo mark (which can be seen on the t-shirts) for responsive uses. Business cards include a subtle gold-foil, which pops out against the dark gray background. Logos were also printed on stickers and other marketing materials.
